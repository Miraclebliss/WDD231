About Localized Problem Solver

Empowering communities to solve local issues together

Our Mission

Localized Problem Solver is a community-driven platform that leverages crowdsourced data to address hyper-local issues in Lagos and beyond. We believe that by working together, communities can identify, report, and track solutions to the problems that affect their daily lives.

How It Works

Our platform enables residents to report issues in their neighborhoods, from power outages and traffic bottlenecks to flooding alerts and sanitation problems. These reports are then visualized on our interactive map, allowing others to see, validate, and contribute to solving these issues.
