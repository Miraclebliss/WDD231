Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the application process take?

Most applications are processed within 2-3 business days. You'll receive a welcome email once approved.

Can I upgrade my membership later?

Yes! You can upgrade at any time. The prorated difference will be applied to your new membership level.

Is there a discount for multiple businesses?

We offer a 10% discount for each additional business under the same ownership.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, and bank transfers. Annual payments receive a 5% discount.
