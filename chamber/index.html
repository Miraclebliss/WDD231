Our Mission

The Milagro Chamber of Commerce is dedicated to promoting and supporting businesses in our region that are committed to ethical employment practices. We believe that businesses thrive when they invest in their employees and communities.

Our mission is to create a network of businesses that prioritize:

  • Fair living wages for all employees
  • Safe and healthy working conditions
  • Inclusive hiring and advancement practices
  • Community investment and development
  • Environmental sustainability

By joining forces, we can create a more equitable and prosperous community for all.
